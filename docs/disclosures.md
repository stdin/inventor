# Public Disclosure History

An append-only log of every public disclosure, sale, demo, issue, commit, and presentation that could touch an invention candidate's novelty or start a filing clock. Log the moment a disclosure happens, not after the fact — pre-filing disclosure can be irreversible for foreign filing rights even where a domestic grace period would otherwise apply (EPC Article 54 treats anything public before filing as prior art).

| Date | Type | Description | Public? | Related candidate(s) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| <YYYY-MM-DD> | commit \| issue \| demo \| talk \| sale \| release \| other | <what was disclosed, and where> | yes/no | `docs/cards/NNNN-slug.md` or "n/a" |

Referenced from `.inventor.json`'s `disclosureLog` field and from each invention card's **Public-disclosure history** field.
